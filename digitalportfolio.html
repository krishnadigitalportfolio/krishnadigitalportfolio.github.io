My SMART Goal

My SMART goal at the beginning of the year was to be an overall better writer.

I feel like throughout the course of the year, I have made small improvements on assignments such as quick writes on SeeSaw, but on my big essays I need to focus more on answering the question. I think some ways I can work on this are providing more analysis and showing that I truly understand what I am writing. Typically I can see errors if I re-re-read my essay so doing that can elp me as well. Throughout the rest of the year, I need to write a lot more. Whether this comes out in the form of poems/short stories I just need to write.

My Strength

My strength was having organized notes and being able to reflect on my notes in the evening. I think I need to use my notes a bit more while doing my homework, because it shows in my tests when I have good notes (i.e. 4.5-4.9 Math Test, Chapter 6 Math Test, Literature quiz Odyssey 1-17). I think my srength hasn't wavered too much throughout the course of the year. I typically take good notes, but it shows when I don't reflect in the evening and don't really use it in my homework. I use my notes on homework for math and my notes are extremely useful for assignments such as quickwrites and quizzes in humanities.

My Stretch

My stretch was improving on being concise in my writing and providing more analysis in my writing. I feel like my stretch has improved on quick writes, but on my bigger essays such as my Scarlet Letter final I feel like I strayed off at one part and didn't really answer the prompt to the best of my capabilities. Other than that, I haven't written any other essays. I think I need to focus more on my essays and I need to focus a bit more on analyzing and answering the question.
